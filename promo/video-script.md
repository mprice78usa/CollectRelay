# CollectRelay Promo Video — Script & Storyboard

**Target length:** 60–90 seconds
**Tone:** Professional, confident, relatable
**Audience:** Real estate agents, accountants, contractors, HR teams

---

## SCENE 1 — Hook (0:00–0:05)

**NARRATION:**
> "You send an email asking for documents. Then another. Then another."

**ON SCREEN:**
- Quick montage: email icons stacking up, "RE: RE: RE: Missing docs" subject lines
- Text overlay fades in: three email subject lines appearing one after another

---

## SCENE 2 — The Problem (0:05–0:12)

**NARRATION:**
> "Wrong file. Wrong format. 'Which ones did I already send?' — collecting documents shouldn't feel like herding cats."

**ON SCREEN:**
- Frustrated emoji or visual of chaotic inbox
- Text: "Collecting documents shouldn't feel like this."

---

## SCENE 3 — Introducing CollectRelay (0:12–0:18)

**NARRATION:**
> "Meet CollectRelay. One link. One portal. Everything organized."

**ON SCREEN:**
- CollectRelay logo animates in
- Tagline appears: "Send requests. Collect files. Stay on track."
- Clean transition to app UI

---

## SCENE 4 — Create a Transaction (0:18–0:30)

**NARRATION:**
> "Start by picking a template — pre-approval docs, tax returns, employee onboarding — whatever you need. Add your client's info, and CollectRelay builds the checklist for you."

**ON SCREEN:**
1. Dashboard with stats cards (Active, In Review, Completed, Total)
2. Click "New Transaction"
3. Template dropdown opens → select "Pre-Approval Package"
4. Sidebar shows checklist preview (numbered items with type badges)
5. Client name + email filled in
6. Click "Create Transaction"

---

## SCENE 5 — Send to Client (0:30–0:36)

**NARRATION:**
> "Hit send — your client gets one secure link. No app to download. No account to create."

**ON SCREEN:**
1. Transaction detail page with "Send to Client" button
2. Button click → email sent confirmation
3. Show email preview arriving: "You have documents to submit" with magic link button

---

## SCENE 6 — Client Portal (0:36–0:50)

**NARRATION:**
> "Clients see exactly what's needed. Upload files by dragging and dropping — or snap a photo on their phone. Answer questions. Sign documents. Everything in one clean portal."

**ON SCREEN:**
1. Client portal opens — clean, simple interface
2. Progress bar: "3 of 8 items complete — 37%"
3. Expand a document item → drag file into upload zone → "Uploaded successfully" ✓
4. Expand a question item → type answer → submit
5. Progress bar updates to 50%
6. Show mobile view briefly (responsive layout)

---

## SCENE 7 — Pro Review Workflow (0:50–1:02)

**NARRATION:**
> "Back on your side, you see everything in real time. Accept documents with one click, or reject with feedback — your client gets notified instantly and knows exactly what to fix."

**ON SCREEN:**
1. Pro transaction detail with submitted items highlighted
2. Click "Accept" on a document → green checkmark
3. Click "Reject" on another → type "Need last 3 months, not just 2" → submit
4. Show comment thread: pro and client exchanging messages
5. Activity dot pulsing on unseen items

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Music:** Upbeat, modern, subtle (royalty-free — try Epidemic Sound or Artlist)
- **Pacing:** Each scene transitions smoothly, no hard cuts
- **Colors:** Match the app's dark theme — dark backgrounds, green accents (#10b981)
- **Font:** Clean sans-serif matching the app (Inter or similar)
- **Recording tip:** Use Loom or OBS to record the animated HTML page, then add voiceover in CapCut, iMovie, or DaVinci Resolve
