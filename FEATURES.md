## Templates — Build Once, Use Forever

Templates are your reusable checklists. Set up what you need once, then launch a new transaction in seconds.

- **Pre-built starter templates** for common real estate transaction types so you don't start from scratch
- **Custom templates** — create your own for any workflow
- **Four item types:**
  - **Documents** — client uploads a file (PDF, JPG, PNG, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX)
  - **Questions** — client types a text answer
  - **Checkboxes** — client acknowledges a statement
  - **E-Signatures** — coming soon
- Mark items as **required or optional**
- Add **descriptions and examples** so clients know exactly what you need
- **Duplicate templates** to quickly create variations
- **Drag and reorder** items to control the flow
- **Preview mode** — see exactly what your client will see before sending

## Transactions — Your Deal Tracker

Every transaction in CollectRelay represents a real deal. Create one from a template, fill in the client details, and send the link.

- **Create from any template** — checklist items are automatically cloned
- **Track deal details:** sale price, commission rate, and commission amount (auto-calculated or manual override)
- **Custom fields** for any extra info — escrow company, loan officer, MLS number, whatever you need
- **Status tracking** through the full lifecycle:
  - **Draft** — setting things up
  - **Active** — sent to the client, collecting documents
  - **In Review** — you're reviewing submissions
  - **Completed** — everything's done
  - **Cancelled** — deal fell through (revivable if it comes back)
- **Due dates** with overdue and due-soon warnings
- **Add items on the fly** — need something extra mid-transaction? Add it without creating a new template
- **Revive cancelled transactions** if a deal comes back to life
- **Permanently delete** cancelled transactions when you're done with them

## Communication — Stay Connected Without the Chase

### Email Notifications
Every important event triggers an email automatically:
- **Client receives:** magic link emails, reminder emails, review notifications (accepted/rejected/changes requested), comment notifications
- **You receive:** submission notifications when clients upload or answer, comment notifications

### SMS Notifications
When you add a client's phone number, CollectRelay sends **text messages alongside emails** — because some clients respond to texts faster than email.
- Magic link texts when you send the portal link
- Reminder texts when you nudge or auto-reminders fire
- Powered by Twilio (requires setup in your account settings)

### Nudge Button
Sometimes you need to send a gentle reminder right now. Hit the **Nudge** button on any active transaction to immediately send a fresh email and text to your client. You'll see when the last reminder was sent so you don't over-message.

### Automated Reminders
Set it and forget it. Configure a **reminder interval** (e.g., every 3 days) and CollectRelay will automatically email and text clients who still have outstanding items. Reminders only go out when there are actually items left to complete.

## Review & Approve — Stay in Control

When clients submit documents or answers, you review them right in CollectRelay.

- **Accept** — mark the item as complete
- **Request Changes** — reject with a note explaining what's needed (client gets notified and can resubmit)
- **Waive** — skip the item entirely if it turns out you don't need it
- **Review notes** — add context when you accept or reject
- **Activity indicators** — see which items have new submissions you haven't looked at yet (orange "new" badges)

## Key Dates & Milestones

Track every important date in your transaction with a visual timeline.

- **Seed default milestones** from a contract date — CollectRelay automatically calculates:
  - Inspection Deadline (10 business days)
  - Appraisal Due (15 business days)
  - Title Review (25 business days)
  - Loan Commitment (30 business days)
  - Final Walkthrough (1 day before closing)
  - Closing (45 business days)
- **Add custom milestones** for anything else you need to track
- **Mark milestones complete** as dates pass
- **Edit dates inline** — dates shift? Update them in one click
- **Add to Calendar** — export milestones as .ics files to Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, or Outlook (individual dates or all at once)

## Partner Portals — Keep Everyone in the Loop

Real estate deals involve more than just you and your client. Share read-only access with the other professionals on the deal.

- **Invite partners** by name and email — choose their role:
  - Lender
  - Title company
  - Co-agent
  - Other
- Partners get a **branded portal link** via email (valid for 30 days)
- Partners see: transaction status, checklist progress, item statuses, and key dates
- Partners **cannot see:** sale price, commission details, or custom fields
- **Revoke access** at any time with one click
